Thriller Anime can be organized under four main sub-genres which are mystery, suspense, psychological, and crime thrillers. If the genre is to be defined you have to understand there is no narrow definition of a thriller genre, but it may be defined by its primary characteristics. The main elements of a thriller Anime is one that promotes an intense rush of emotions such as apprehension, a high level of anticipation, suspense, fearful excitement, heightened expectations, ambiguity, exhilaration, breathlessness, uncertainty, anxiety and nerve-wracking tension all in the attempt to hold the interest of the audience by keeping them alert and on the edge of their seats as the plot builds towards a climax.
